About Access Real Estate

We develop premium accessible housing in Cairo and Giza, serving people with disabilities, older adults, and families who need barrier-free living without sacrificing elegance. Our designs pair accessibility with craftsmanship, informed by advisors and lived experience to exceed requirements by 25%.

Mission

To build quality accessible homes that improve life for people with disabilities while making profit for our business.

Vision

To be the top company known for building accessible housing that helps people with disabilities live independently.

What sets us apart

  • Accessibility reviews that go 25% beyond legal standards.
  • Layouts customized for mobility, sensory comfort, and daily routines.
  • Community integration with advisory input from people with disabilities.
  • Premium materials, calm aesthetics, and discreet smart-home options.

Who we serve

People with disabilities, older adults, and families in Egypt seeking accessible, dignified homes with thoughtful support for independence.

Accessible and premium, without compromise

Six essential features crafted into every residence, with options to tailor details to your needs.

Wide circulation

Generous pathways and doorways for effortless movement with mobility devices.

Step-free access

Zero-threshold entries, lifts, and gentle transitions from arrival to every room.

Roll-in showers

Barrier-free bathing with secure grab support and slip-resistant finishes.

Accessible kitchens

Thoughtful work zones with adaptable counters, clear reach ranges, and organized storage.

Smart-home options

Accessible controls, lighting, and climate settings that respond to your preferences.

Open layouts

Expansive plans with calm sightlines, premium finishes, and generous turning space.
